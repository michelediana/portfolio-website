Fitted

Fitted is a fitness application to help users to achieve their fitness goals. This app was designed for individuals who have a busy life schedule or who are new to fitness.

Objective

Motivate people into an exercise routine that suits their level, schedule, and interests.

Role

MI lead in designing the user interface and the visual aesthetic of the application. Using the project, I was able to communicate the feeling of joy and happiness that the client wanted to invoke.

1 Low-Fidelity

I started my process with low-fidelity wireframes to understand how the text, UI elements and overall design of the application layouts.

2 User Flows

Created user flows where I can understand how the users will navigate through pages to achieve their tasks.

3 Med-Fidelity Grid

By creating med-fidelity wireframes I was able to view how elements will be laid out in the app and understand the spacing of each element.

4 Med-Fidelity

By creating this fidelity wireframe I was focused on the hierarchy of the layout. Figuring out how to establish where the users should focus their attention first and help the users navigate their eyes up and down each page.

5 High-Fidelity Wireframes

When creating these wireframes I used colors that were listed in the design brief which was orange and black. I used a lot of different shades of orange to envoke joy and happiness throughout the application.

6 Revision Fidelity Wireframes

I redesigned the color scheme of the application after viewing the app. Replacing the black background with white let the orange pop out more and gave the feeling of more happiness and joy.

7 MoodBoard

When designing the mood board, I used images that are uplifting and joyful. Fitted gives users motivation toward fitness and encouragement.

8 Responsive Design Mockups

Designing for tablet and desktop I stayed consistent with feel and placement of the mobile design, causing less friction for users to achieve their objectives when attempting their tasks.
