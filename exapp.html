Ex App

Are you looking to move further in your career or making a career change? You will want guidance, advice, and tips from real experts in those fields. Ex App provides real experts with years of experience and knowledge in their careers helping individuals jump to the next level in their jobs.

Objective

Enable anyone, anywhere to instantly chat with an expert in virtually any field.

Role

My role on this project was to be the UX designer. I conducted user interviews, user research used the best practices, collected data from research information, prioritized content, created wireframes, developed prototypes for user testings and designed user-interface.

1 Problem Statement

I began this project by creating a problem statement. I used the double diamond strategy to start my research process and understand what problems I will face when building this application. The lessons I learned from this step was how to point out pain points in applications and approach this project with empathy for potential users.

2 Competitive Analysis

I conducted competitive research from other apps and websites to observe what other competitors are doing. I found their strengths and marketing strategies, and this allowed me to focus on areas where users are motivated to complete their tasks. In this task, I learned how to dive deep into a competitor's website and application to understand how to dissect them into a chart where I can view an entire break down of each website of applications. This step was vital to my process, and I was able to understand what worked well and how to improve in weak areas to satisfy users' needs. 

3 User Personas

Collecting Data analysis, user interviews, and research information I was able to create user personas which reflects my target audience.

4 User Flows

I created user flows to understand how users would use the app and how to create the least amount of friction as possible.

5 Information Architect

From the results of my user tests, I’m able to create an information architecture where a user would cognitively find pages in the app without friction and motivate them to complete their tasks.

6 Wireframes

I created med-fidelity using Balsamiq to gain a visual where elements of the app will be placed. Later I created a hi-fidelity to understand the visual elements and observed how the elements would work together.

7 User Testing

After creating my prototype, I tested the app with real users to understand their pain points and what motivates them. Conducted interviews in person using my iPhone 7 to record the interviews where I was able to view the user’s facial expressions during the interview. I used InVision app for my prototype and the app worked very well. I was able to share the prototype with the participants which made the interviews run a lot smoother.

8 Style Guide

After the test results, I analyzed the feedback from the users and I corrected the pain points that the users were having and created a design guide to determine the style of the app.

9 Final Design

Initial research showed blue is color which evokes trust with the users. I decided to user navy color blue and tints of lighter blue to envoke the feeling of trust throughout the application. Allowing the users to feel safe with the decision to sign up with the Ex App.
